About This Car

BMW X6 Price: BMW X6 price starts at Rs. 1.04 Crore and goes upto Rs. 1.11 Crore. The price of Petrol variant for X6 ranges between Rs. 1.04 Crore - Rs. 1.11 Crore.
BMW X6 Versions: X6 is available in 6 variants. All the variants are Automatic (TC).
BMW X6 Colours: X6 is offered in 8 colours: Alpine White, Carbon Black Metallic, Mineral White Metallic, Manhattan Metallic, Flamenco Red Brilliant Effect Metallic, Arctic Grey Brilliant Effect Metallic, Sophisto Grey Brilliant Effect Metallic and Black Sapphire metallic. However, some of these colours are available in specific versions.
BMW X6 Competitors: X6 is competing against BMW X5, Audi Q8 e-tron, Lexus RX, Mercedes-Benz GLE, Mercedes-Benz EQC, Audi e-tron, BMW iX, Jaguar I-Pace and Audi Q8.
